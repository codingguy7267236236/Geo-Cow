Trade Blocs

NAFTA

Members

  • United States of America
  • Canada
  • Mexico

Purpose

  • Reduce tariffs and promote trade in North America

Pros

  • No tariff trades
  • Cheaper trade
  • Increased investment to Mexico and Canada
  • 1000s of jobs for Canada and Mexico

Cons

  • America went from $1.6bn goods trade surplus with Mexico to $70bn trade defecit
  • USA's goods trade deficit grew from $115bn to $800bn
  • Economic carnage for USA as jobs offshored to Mexico

OPEC - Organization of Petroleum Exporting Countries

Members

  • Algeria
  • Angola
  • Congo
  • Equatorial Guinea
  • Gabon
  • Iran
  • Irag
  • Kuwait
  • Libya
  • Nigeria
  • Saudi Arabia
  • UAE - United Arab Emirates
  • Venezuela

Purpose

  • Co-ordinate and unify petroleum policies among Member Countries
  • Secure fair and stable prices for petroleum producers
  • Supply regular supply of petroleum to consuming nations
  • Produce fair return on capital to investors

Pros

  • Regular Supplies of petroleum
  • Affordable prices for petroleum
  • Limits exploitation of member nations

Cons

  • Harming global economy - policies limit production leading to higher prices
  • Infaltion - due to high oil prices
  • Geo-political issues can affect supply
  • Differences in ideologies across member nations

Global agreements

Paris Accords

Members

  • 196 countries
  • United Kingdom - pays $5.6bn
  • France
  • USA - pays $3bn

Purpose

  • Limit global warming to 1.5°C
  • Strengthen countries' ability to deal with the impacts of climate change

Pros

  • Aims to reduce global warming
  • "International support"
  • "Holds big emitters accountable"

Cons

  • Developed countries provide financial aid to developing
  • Developed countries expected to provide assistance to developing countries via resources and finaces.
  • Fails to address "free riders" - nations taking advantage of benefits without contribution

Facts

International Whaling Commision

Facts

  • International governmental organisation
  • Participation is voluntary

Pros

  • Most countries observe the moratorium
  • Blue stocks slowly recovered in southern ocean
  • Sets quotas for hunting certain Whale species

Cons

  • Not all countries observe moratorium
  • 935 minks still being killed
  • Japan doesn't observe moratorium and continues hunting whales under "scientific research"
