Gazprom - Russia

Facts

  • Pipeline goes through Ukraine, Poland, Kazakhstan & Turkey.
  • 51% controlled by Russian Government.
  • Germany and Europe dependent on Russian Gas.
  • Conflict with Ukraine causing shortages leading to high prices & high cost of living.
  • Ukraine wanting to join NATO led to Russia cutting supplies
  • Controls 17% of world gas reserves.
  • Infrastruture ugly and damages landscape.

Hydroelectric energy - Norway

Facts

  • Provides 95% Norweigan energy
  • Commissioned 35 new hydropower stations in 2016, providing 154MW
  • Plans for interconnectors to UK to transport excess hydroelectric energy
  • Integrated open electricity market with Sweden, Denmark & Finland
  • Relies on Nord Pool so any Nordic nations cutting ties threatens energy security

Coca Cola in Kerala (India)

Facts

  • Took 510k litres each day
  • Over abstracted Keralas supply - water table fell 260ft
  • Operations contaminated water supplies
  • Coke subsides for water irrigation scheme
  • No water sources caused harvests to drop 40%

Aral Sea

Facts

  • Uzbekistan & Kazakstan conflict due to cotton and fish farming imapcts due to scarcity of water respectfully
  • High evaporation rates since 1960 due to over abstraction of the area
  • Lack of water has led to 75 per 1000 infant mortality rates
  • Dam built to control water flow & planted more trees to shelter soil (less drying up)
  • Retreating sea led to fishing industry decline

London Basin

Facts

  • Over abstraction from brewing industry led to water levels dropped 88m below sea level
  • 2.6bn litres for 9m population
  • 250mm of 690mm useable rainfall
  • Water levels recovering 3m per year since 1990 due to less economic activity
  • Mid 60s brewing industry replaced by commerce industry

Three Gorges Dam

Facts

  • Alleviating drying North via transport scheme
  • Increased water supplies leading to improved leisure activities
  • Stopped migration of dolphins when built
  • Residents forcefully relocated to make way for dam
  • Dam predicted to cause earthquakes & landslides

Chuqicamata (Chile)

Facts

  • China consumes 80% of Chiles copper
  • Town used to be home to 25k people before used for mining
  • Pollution from mines so severe area was deemed unsafe for human habitation
  • Refining process emits large quantities of particulate matter & sulphur oxides
  • 400k tonnes of waste removed daily, some poured into rivers
